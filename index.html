About Me

Hi! My name is George Flores. I was born in Long Island, NY and moved to North Carolina around 2005. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNC Charlotte) with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and a minor in Communication Studies. Previously, I worked as a tire technician at Discount Tire while attending UNC Charlotte and have worked there for about 7 years. Currently, I am attending the Coding Bootcamp at UNC Charlotte in pursuit of a developer role so I can break into the technology field.

My hobbies include video games and watching shows on Netflix! I enjoy playing adventure and role-playing games like the Legend of Zelda and Final Fantasy series. Also, I love watching fantasy and animated like The Last Kingdom and Castlevania.
